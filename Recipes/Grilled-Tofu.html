Grilled Tofu For Days

This recipe guides you in making the best grilled tofu of your life!

Are you vegan? Are you not vegan but you just like tofu? Well have I got the recipe for you! This grilled tofu recipe is great for lunch or dinner to accompany a main dish!

You ready? Here we go!

INGREDIENTS
  • Firm tofu (As much or little as you want)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sweet paprika to taste
  • Pepper to toaste
  • Olive oil

Got your ingredients? Here we go!

INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Cut the tofu into strips with a thickness of about 1/4" (.5-1cm).
  2. As an optional step, pat the strips with a paper towel to get out some extra moisture.
  3. In a deep plate, put about 2tbs of soy sauce, set the strips of tofu in the soy sauce, turning them over.
  4. Sprinkle some sweet paprika and pepper over the strips to taste.
  5. While letting the tofu soak for a couple of minutes, heat up a pan and put enough olive oil that it's lightly covering the base of the pan.
  6. Once the oil is hot, set the strips on the pan and cook them over medium-high heat until you're satisfied with their crispiness.
  7. Enjoy!

I hope you enjoyed the results of this recipe. Make your own changes to customize your grilled tofu!
