The Best Popcorn You'll Ever Have

Want salty, butter, delicious popcorn? You've come to the right place!

Popcorn is one of the healthiest snacks out there, and today we're going to make some so later you can munch away while working, watching a movie, or just about anything else!

Here are the ingredients you'll need:

Ingredients
  • Popcorn kernels (about 3/4-1 cup)
  • 3Tbs of butter (substitue for olive oil or sunflower oil)
  • Salt

Got your ingredients? Here we go!

Instructions
  1. First were going to put our butter in a wide pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Toss in a couple of kernels along with the butter.
  3. Once the kernels pop, add in the rest.
  4. Cover your pot and move your pot above the heated stovetop, this way your popcorn doesn't burn.
  5. Once you hear the popcorn slow down or stop popping completely lower the heat
  6. Let your popcorn cool down a bit before digging in
  7. Enjoy!
